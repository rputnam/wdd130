I love nature! I always have since I can remember. Being in nature helps me feel connected to God. I love being outside and seeing the beauty of the trees and the flowers. I am always amazed and mesmerized by the detail and the colors I see! When I stop and look at God's creations around me, I feel a strong sense of reverence and awe for the beauty that surrounds me. The beauty of the earth is a gift and a retreat from the loud and confusing world. The sense of peace and comfort that washes over me when I am in nature helps me to feel grounded, renewed and a deep spiritual connection to my Heavenly Father.

“It is that the Savior indeed was the creator of heaven and earth, and that He is the Divine Pattern after whom we should fashion our lives.”
