About Me

Most of the art pieces I have created and shared are of realistic botanical watercolor paintings. I first began to experiment with colored pencils and pastels when I was a child. So I decided to give it a try again and created an original piece using only colored pencils. The American Flag is an important symbol of freedom and I have titled it “In God We Trust”. I have also begun to branch out and have painted other pieces that like this pumpkin. With this pumpkin I used watercolor paints.
